
Steps in building a time series forecast with SARIMAX
1. load, clean and transform the data.
2. test of randomness
3. test for stationarity
4. perform feature engineering
5. stationarize the time series data if it is not staionary
6. split the data to train and test set
7. add exogenous variables to the SARIMAX model
8. fit the model and make predictions
9. compare prediciton with the actual values
10. evaluate your predicitons
